ℹ️ About JanSankalpAI

Empowering citizens and government through Artificial Intelligence for transparent, efficient, and smarter public grievance resolution.

🎯 Our Mission

JanSankalpAI aims to bridge the gap between citizens and government by providing an intelligent platform where people can report public issues, track their progress, and ensure faster resolution through AI-assisted analysis.

🚀 What JanSankalpAI Offers

  • AI-powered complaint validation and analysis.
  • Support for text, image, and voice complaints.
  • Automatic department assignment.
  • Real-time complaint tracking.
  • Government dashboard for efficient grievance management.
  • Community support and complaint prioritization.
  • Multilingual accessibility for citizens.

🌍 Our Vision

We envision a future where every citizen can easily communicate local issues, governments can make data-driven decisions, and communities become cleaner, safer, and more responsive through the responsible use of Artificial Intelligence.

🤖 Why Artificial Intelligence?

AI helps identify duplicate complaints, classify issues, estimate severity, generate summaries, suggest actions, and prioritize complaints for government officials. Final decisions are always made by authorized government officers.
