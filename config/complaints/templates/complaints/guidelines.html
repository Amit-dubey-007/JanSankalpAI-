📖 Citizen Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines to help us process your complaint quickly and accurately.

Before You Submit

  • Use a clear and meaningful complaint title.
  • Describe the issue in detail.
  • Provide the correct location and department.
  • Upload clear photos or voice recordings whenever possible.
  • Avoid submitting duplicate complaints.

AI Review Process

Every complaint is first reviewed by AI for validation and categorization. AI assists government officers, but all final decisions are made by authorized officials.

Complaint Status

Pending
Complaint has been received.
In Progress
Government department is working on it.
Resolved
Complaint has been successfully resolved.
Rejected / Invalid
Complaint was rejected or did not meet platform guidelines.

Community Rules

  • Submit only genuine public issues.
  • Do not upload fake or misleading information.
  • Avoid offensive or abusive language.
  • Respect other citizens and government officials.

Important Notice

False complaints, spam, abusive content, or repeated misuse of the platform may result in warnings, temporary account suspension, or permanent blocking.
